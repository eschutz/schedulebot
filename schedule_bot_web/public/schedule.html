Usage Description
schedule from dd/mm/yy hh:mm to dd/mm/yy hh:mm event Schedule a once-only event between two dates
schedule weekly day hh:mm to day hh:mm activity Schedule a regular (weekly) event
schedule view View your schedule
schedule view more View your schedule and the corresponding event IDs
schedule preset preset_name ENABLE/DISABLE Adds the specified preset to your schedule (see presets)
schedule clear [YES] Removes all events on your schedule
schedule timezone [city] View and set your timezone to the specified city.
schedule remove ID Remove the specified event from your schedule
