:calendar: **ScheduleBot:** A simple bot to let others know where you are and when you are busy.

**Visit the website!**
sch-bot.herokuapp.com

__Execute each command by itself to display in-depth help.__

Commands:

`&info`
      Display this help dialog.

`&welcome`
      An easy introduction and tutorial to ScheduleBot.

`&where` **[username]**
      Find out what a user is currently doing.

`&schedule` **[from {date} {time} to {date} {time} {activity}]**
      Set your schedule for the specified day/time. This will be displayed to other users when they query your username with `&where`.
